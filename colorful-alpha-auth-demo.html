How It Works
THE MECHANISM
01
Initial Selection
User selects their first name initial (A–Z) and last name initial (A–Z). 26 × 26 = 676 base combinations.
02
Color Assignment
Each initial is paired with a personally memorable HEX color from a curated palette. Colors are registered during enrollment.
03
Token Generation
Letter + HEX pair is hashed into a 128-bit authentication token. Optionally layered with biometric or PIN for multi-factor.
04
Verification
Server-side comparison without storing raw credentials. Works for elderly users, children (NISA), and low-literacy contexts.
